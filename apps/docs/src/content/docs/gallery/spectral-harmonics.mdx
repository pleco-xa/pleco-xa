Once you know an instrument's fundamental, its overtones sit at integer
multiples of it. This demo tracks a fundamental that glides from 110 Hz to 165
Hz, then samples the spectrogram at that moving f0 and its harmonics. The
harmonic amplitudes follow the expected 1/h falloff, and the f0-normalized view
stays coherent even as the raw pitch slides — the whole point of harmonic
tracking over fixed-frequency bins.

<DemoEmbed src="plot-spectral-harmonics.html" title="Harmonic spectrum tracking" height={760} />

Pitch comes from `yin`; the overtone sampling is `f0_harmonics` evaluated on FFT
frequencies. Good voice-range parameters are `fmin` 50 / `fmax` 300 with
harmonics 1..30.
